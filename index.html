Things to Do in Jinan

Jinan is known as the "City of Springs." Here are a few highlights:

  • Baotu Spring Park: (On your itinerary!) Famous for its gushing spring, considered one of China's best.
  • Daming Lake: (On your itinerary!) A large, beautiful lake in the city center, great for a stroll or boat ride.
  • Black Tiger Spring (Heihuquan): (On your itinerary!) Watch locals fill up jugs with fresh spring water.
  • Thousand Buddha Mountain (Qianfo Shan): Offers great panoramic views of the city and features many Buddhist carvings.
  • Furong Street (Furongjie): A bustling, narrow street perfect for trying local snacks and street food.
  • Shandong Museum: A large, modern museum with impressive historical and cultural artifacts.
